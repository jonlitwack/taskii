THE WOVEN CHRONICLE MARK

The Chronicle Hotels logo mark is inspired by diagonal weave patterns found in traditional textiles across cultures worldwide. The crossed diagonal lines within a circular frame represent the intersection of stories, cultures, and craft traditions that define each Chronicle location.

  • Diagonal Weaving: References authentic textile craftsmanship from indigenous traditions to contemporary artisan work
  • Layered Depth: Primary and secondary lines create visual depth, suggesting the multiple layers of meaning in every story
  • Circular Containment: The circle represents completeness, community, and the globe‚ÄîChronicle hotels as destinations that contain entire worlds
  • Cultural Authenticity: Unlike generic luxury symbols, this mark directly connects to the hands-on craft traditions that inform Chronicle's design philosophy

BRAND ESSENCE

Chronicle Hotels captures the living narrative of place. Each property is a curator of local stories, where authentic cultural heritage meets sophisticated hospitality.
